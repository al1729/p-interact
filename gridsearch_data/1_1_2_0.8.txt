The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.


As she walked through the town, people stopped and stared at her. She didn't know why, but it made her feel uneasy. When she got home, she went to her bedroom and looked in the mirror. That's when she saw it: a small goldfish swimming around in her head.

At first she thought it was some kind of trick or illusion, but no matter how much she rubbed her eyes or shook her head, the fish stayed put. She wondered what could have caused such a thing to happen, but no matter how much she racked her brain, she couldn't think of anything.

The next day wasn't any better; if anything, things were even stranger. The townsfolk now pointed and laughed whenever they saw her walking by - even children mockedher mercilessly! And as for the fish? It seemed to be getting bigger...
