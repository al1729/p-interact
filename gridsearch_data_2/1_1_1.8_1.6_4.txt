The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.


The girl woke up to the sound of birds chirping outside her window. It was a beautiful day, and she couldn't help but feel happy despite herself. She got out of bed, stretched, and went to look outside.

What she saw took her breath away. The sky was a deep blue, not a cloud in sight. The sun was shining so bright it almost hurt her eyes to look at it. And in the distance she could see the outline of a city - something she had never seen before from her small town home.
She felt like today was going to be different; like something amazing was going to happen. And sure enough, by the end of the day, everything had changed for this small-town girl...
