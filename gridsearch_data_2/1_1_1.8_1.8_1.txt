The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.
 She woke up feeling like something big was going to happen. And sure enough, by the time she went to bed that night, her life had changed forever.

She had always been fascinated by the stream that ran through town. It wasn't very big, but it was deep and there were always fish swimming in it. That day, she decided to go explore it a bit more. She followed the stream until she came to a spot where it widened and fed into a small pond. It was here that she saw him for the first time.

He was different than any of the other boys in town; he seemed larger than life somehow. He jumped into the water with ease and started swim across the pond towards her. She felt drawn to him in a way she couldn't explain and when he reached shore, they simply looked at each other for what felt like an eternity before finally speaking.."
