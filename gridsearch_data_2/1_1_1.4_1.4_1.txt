The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.
 She woke up feeling excited, as if something great was about to happen. And sure enough, by the time she went to bed that night, her life had changed forever.

It started with a phone call from a friend she hadn't talked to in months. They catching up for a while and then her friend told her about a new opportunity that was perfect for her. She hung up the phone feeling motivated and inspired for the first time in months. It felt like finally something was happening in her life worth getting excited about.

The next day, she took action on what her friend had told her and applied for the job. To her surprise, she got it! Suddenly, she was thrust into a whole new world of excitement and adventure. She loved every minute of it and quickly made friends in this new environment. From then on, things just kept getting better and better until she realized that this boring small-town girl's life had turnedinto something truly magical.*
