The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.
 The girl woke up with a sense of excitement, as if something wonderful was about to happen. And sure enough, by the time she went to bed that night, her life had changed forever.

It all started when she received a letter in the mail. She didn't recognize the handwriting, but it was addressed to her nonetheless. eagerly, she opened it and found that inside was an invitation to an event that sounded like nothing she'd ever heard of before. It sparked her curiosity and so she decided to go.

That evening, she put on her best dress and made her way to the address listed on the invite. When she arrived,she found herself in front of a large mansion - certainly not something you would find in their small town! Hesitantly,she knocked on the door and waited until somebody answered..
