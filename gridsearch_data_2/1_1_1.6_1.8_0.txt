The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.
 She woke up feeling more alive than ever before, and she had no idea why. She decided to take advantage of this sudden energy by finally doing something she'd always wanted to do: exploring the woods behind her house.

She had always been told not to go into the woods, as they were supposedly dangerous, but she had never really believed that. After all, what could be so bad about a few trees? But now that she was actually in the woods, she started to feel a bit uneasy. It was much darker and scarier than she remembered it being. She heard strange noises all around her and began to worry that maybe her parents were right after all.

Just as she was about turn back though,she saw something glimmering in the distance. Curiosity getting the better of her,she slowly made her way towards it. And when she got closer,she realized that it was a fairy! The most beautiful creature she had ever seen. They looked at each other for a moment before the fairy flew away deeper into the woodlands leavingthe girl standing there in awe
