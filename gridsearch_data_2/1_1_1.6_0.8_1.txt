The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.
 She awoke to the most beautiful sunrise she had ever seen. The colors were so vibrant and alive that it took her breath away. And as she watched the sun slowly rise in the sky, she felt a sense of wonder and possibility stirring within her. For the first time in a long time, she felt truly alive.

As the day went on, this feeling only grew stronger. She found herself noticing all of the little things around her that she had always taken for granted before: The way the leaves rustled in the breeze, how each person's face was unique and interesting, even how delicious her dinner tasted. Everything seemed new and full of possibility.

That night, as she lay in bed staring up at the stars, she knew that something inside of her had changed forever. She was no longer content to just live a boring life--she wanted to explore and experience all that life had to offer. Andshe would start tomorrow morning with a brand new outlook on everything...
