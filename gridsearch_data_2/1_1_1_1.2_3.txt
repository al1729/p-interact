The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.


As she went about her usual routine, she couldn’t help but feel as though something was watching her. It was a feeling she had never experienced before, and it made her skin crawl. She tried to shake it off, but the feeling only got stronger as the day went on.

By the time night had fallen, she was convinced that something was definitely following her. She could feel its presence lurking in the shadows, stalking her every move. Her heart racing, she ran to the safety of her home and barricaded herself inside.

She didn’t know what it was or why it was after her, but she knew that whatever it was, it wasn’t going to give up until it got what it wanted. And she didn’t intend to be its next victim…
