The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.
 She woke up feeling as if something exciting was going to happen. And sure enough, by the time she went to bed that night, her life had changed forever.

It started with a small thing, a change in routine. She decided to take the scenic route to school instead of the usual shortcut. This led her to walking past the old abandoned house that everyone said was haunted. As she walked past it, she felt a chill go down her back and quickened her pace. But then she heard something - a faint sound coming from inside the house...
