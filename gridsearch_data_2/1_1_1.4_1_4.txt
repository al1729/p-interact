The life of a small-town girl only ever seemed to be one of boredom and simplicity. She would wake up each day, do the same things she always did, and go to bed with nothing new having happened. But on this particular day, something was different.
 She could feel it in the air.

As she made her way through her daily routine, she couldn’t help but notice that everyone seemed to be on edge. The normally chatty Cashier was silent as he rung up her groceries. Even the birds in the sky seemed to be singing a different tune.

She got home and found her dog, Barkley, barking furiously at the front door. She had never seen him so worked up before. She slowly approached the door, wondering what could possibly be outside that had Barkley so upset.

She cautiously opened the door and peered outside. At first, she saw nothing out of the ordinary. But then she noticed a small figure huddled in the corner of her porch. It was a little girl, crying and shaking uncontrollably..

 The girl looked up at her with pleading eyes and said softly, “Please help me…they’re coming after me…”
